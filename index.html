How to Play

🏁 The Objective

Navigate from a Start Article to a Target Article using only the links within the Wikipedia pages!

  • Solo Mission: Play at your own pace and earn Rank MMR.
  • Multiplayer: Race against a friend using a Lobby Code.
  • Coins (🪙): Earned by winning. Spend 2 coins on "🎲 Randomize" to re-roll difficult words before you start.
  • Profile (Top Left): Check your stats and save/load your progress!

🎮 Game Modes

Use the ← and → arrows in the menu to change modes!

  • Classic: Standard difficulty. Finding the fastest path is key.
  • Hardcore: Obscure, difficult topics. High risk, high reward.
  • Daily: A unique challenge every 24h. Build your streak (🔥)!
  • 10 Clicks: Reach the target in 10 clicks or less. Efficiency is everything!

📊 MMR Rewards

MMR is gained based on your efficiency compared to the shortest possible path:

+1 Click: +250
+2 Clicks: +150
+3 Clicks: +95
+4 Clicks: +60
+5 Clicks: +40

✨ Complexity Bonus: Win harder topics (Hardcore/Master+) to earn up to +150% extra MMR per win!

🏆 Ranks & Divisions

Climb from Bronze all the way to Legend!

The Division System

Each Tier (Bronze, Silver, Gold, etc.) is split into 5 Divisions. You start at Division 5 and work your way up to Division 1 to reach the next Tier.

Promotion Matches

Once you hit the MMR cap of Division 1, you enter a PROMO. To advance to the next higher Tier, you must win a game in "10 Clicks" mode!

Note: High ranks (Platinum+) will lose MMR for giving up or performing poorly!
